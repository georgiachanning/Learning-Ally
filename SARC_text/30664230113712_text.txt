By February 1 of each year, every school in California is required by state law to publish a School Accountability Report 
Card (SARC). The SARC contains information about the condition and performance of each California public school. Under 
the Local Contr
ol Funding Formula (LCFF) all local educational agencies (LEAs) are required to prepare a Local Control and 
Accountability Plan (LCAP), which describes how they intend to meet annual school
-
specific goals for all pupils, with 
specific activities to address
 
state and local priorities. Additionally, data reported in an LCAP is to be consistent with data 
reported in the SARC.
 
 

 
For more information about SARC requirements, see the California Department of Education (CDE) SARC web page at 
https://www.cde.ca.gov/ta/ac/sa/
.
 

 
For more information about the LCFF or LCAP, see the CDE LCFF web page at 
http
s://www.cde.ca.gov/fg/aa/lc/
.
 

 
For additional information about the school, parents/guardians and community members should contact the school 
principal or the district office.

The mission of Orange Grove is to maximize the academic potential of all students to develop confident and responsible

At Orange Grove, our students are responsible and academically successful in a rigorous, enriching, and engaging learning

School Profile
 
Orange Grove Elementary School serves approximately 630 
students in TK through grade six. 
 
In 2018, a TK Dual Language 
Immersion (DLI) classroom in Spanish was started. The DLI instructional program will continue to grow as students 
promote from grade to grade. 
 
It was established in 2006 and currently has a ce

counselor. 
 
In addition to administration, there is a part
-

-
time Behavioral Health

Our instructional focus is on utilizing Explicit Direct Instruction and scaffolding strategies that support a

-
rounded 
curriculum. 
 
There is an emphasis on developing language and problem solving skills by providing ample opportunities for

Maps effectively and Write from the Beginning and Beyond. 
 
We are in the process of establishing a strong focus on using

respect, responsibility, and safety. 
 
 
In 2018 Orange Grove fully committed to implementing STEAM instructional programs and practices that are integrated 
throughout the cur

Our students and staff are active participants in the 100 Mile Club. 
 
 
Identified Gifted and Talented (GATE) students in grades 3
