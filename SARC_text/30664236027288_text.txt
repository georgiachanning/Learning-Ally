Parents at Barton School are given multiple opportunities to actively participate and be involved in their child's

Barton has an

within their child's classroom. Parents can assist the teacher by completing specific activities, tutor students who are 
struggling, or complete other tasks a

In addition Barton School has an active PTA. Barton has a large 
contingency of parents who participate in the monthly meetings as well as volunteer their time at assemblies and other 
activities.
 
 
Our School Site Council is another way in which o
ur parents utilize their leadership skills and expertise. 
Parents are given multiple opportunities to provide input on student safety, instruction, and general concerns regarding 
the school site and their child's education. Parent trainings on various topi
cs, Coffee Chats, or Classroom walk throughs 
are offered monthly. The Parent Leadership institute and Parent 10 Commandment classes are offered yearly in which 
our parent leaders facilitate the learning.
